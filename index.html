Step 1 of 4 — Choose a photo

How to Submit Your Photos

1
Tap "Choose or snap a photo"
Tap the upload area on the main screen. You can select a photo from your camera roll or take a new one with your camera.
2
Crop your photo into a square
Drag and pinch to position your photo inside the square frame. Make sure the most important part of your image is centred. Tap "Use this crop" when happy.
3
Fill up to 12 photo slots
You'll see a grid of 12 slots. Tap any empty slot to add another photo. You can add between 1 and 12 photos — duplicate photos are fine if you want extras of the same magnet. Tap × on any photo to remove and replace it.
4
Tap "Select Photos" to continue
Once you've added at least one photo, the "Select Photos" button appears at the bottom. Tap it to prepare your order.
5
Enter your details and submit
Fill in your full name and contact info (email or phone number). Add any special notes if needed, then tap "Submit Photos." You'll get a confirmation and we'll be in touch with your order details!
